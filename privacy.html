Acceptance of Privacy Policy

The Wrestling Federation of Singapore recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of all information provided by the visitors to our Web site, recipients of our e-mail newsletters, and our members and business associates. The WFS requests your contact information so that we may communicate with you about wrestling news, events, products and services.

The WFS will not share your private information with any other individual or group not directly associated with our federation without your express permission.
