Wrestling Federation of Singapore

The Wrestling Federation of Singapore (WFS) is a National Sports Association that was established on the 18th of December 2008 with Institution of Public Character (IPC) status since the 1st of February 2012.

WFS is the sole governing body for the sport, responsible for the education and promotion of the sport to schools, corporations and local communities.

Although being one of the youngest nations in one of the oldest sports in the world, WFS strives to produce homegrown athletes who have and will continue to achieve success at the international stage, contributing towards our Nation’s sporting achievements.


Programs that WFS runs

  • Beat Our Streets Singapore (Youth-at-risk engagement)
  • Grassroots Wrestling Clubs (People’s Association)
  • Affiliate programs with Commercial MMA Gyms
  • Sports Education Program (SEP)
  • School Interest Groups
  • High Performance National Team

Beat Our Streets Singapore (B.O.S.S)

B.O.S.S is a non-profit programme driven by the Wrestling Federation of Singapore which aims to use the sport of wrestling to inspire our urban children and teenagers and provide them with the academic and athletic support required to reach success.

Through wrestling, participants learn key values such as resilience, discipline, commitment, leadership and sportsmanship. Wrestling also serves as a metaphor for our young on how they can overcome adversity in challenging situations and how the struggle against seemingly unsurmountable odds in wrestling will give them a chance of success.

Through the programme, participants are also equipped with life-skills such as financial literacy. B.O.S.S currently runs free wrestling classes for children and teens under the age of 18 as well as after school programs at various institutions and hopes to further support them with the help of donors and volunteers.



B.O.S.S Programs

  • SG wrestling grading system
  • Financial literacy
  • Values-driven education
  • Free classes for children and teens under 18

Past B.O.S.S Events

TOUCH Community Services, Young Arrows

Beneficiary of the first Wrestling Federation of Singapore's, Beat Our Streets Singapore - Wrestle with a heart fund raiser event.

38 wrestlers (kids and adults) participated in the day long event. The event saw a total of 46 bouts of wrestling action along with charity sale of snacks and beverages. All proceeds from event registrations and sales from snacks and beverages goes to our beneficiary.

(TOUCH Young Arrows helps children aged six to 12 years old from low-income and/or single-parent families through programmes such as free academic coaching in primary school subjects (English and Mathematics), values-driven activities and year-round family bonding events.)

Singapore National Wrestling Team

Our pioneer National Team was established in 2009 and during that year under the tutelage of 2 times Olympic Champion and 6 times World Champion, Sergei Beloglazov, achieved our first ever Silver medal in the SEA games.

Since then, we have achieved medals for our Nation at many regional and international competitions. Our most recent achievement was from Alvina Lim who clinched a Bronze medal at the 2016 Commonwealth Wrestling Championships.

As part of Team Singapore, we hope to continue to do our small nation proud and achieve higher standards at the international stage.
