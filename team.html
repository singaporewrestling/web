Patrick Liew

President

Bernard Chin was the Executive Director of UBS Singapore. He ran Short-Duration sales in Asia. He has spent 13 years at UBS spanning sales, trading and technology in New York, Chicago, London, Hong Kong and Singapore. He is currently a board member of Asian Securities Industry and Financial Markets Association (ASIFMA) and promotes development of liquidity, funding, and repo & securities lending markets in Asia. He is also co-founder of custom sports car manufacturer BC Racing LLP in Singapore. Bernard graduated with BS in Management and MS in Electrical Engineering from MIT, and an MBA from University of Chicago.

Baron is a permanent resident and senior architect at a local architectural firm. He is a passionate advocate of wrestling as a tool for developing the youth. Baron Villanueva

Honorary Secretary

Aaron Koh Sheng Min is the Head of Department of the Physical Education and Co curricula Activities at Radin Mas Primary School, a local government school in the south of Singapore.He has a strong background in curriculum design and pedagogy given his formal training as an educator as well as in the delivery of PE lessons. He was instructed by Mr Sergey Beloglazov for his NCAP Technical Level 1 & 2 certification. He also holds the level 3 NCAP theory certification which he attained during his formative years in teacher training. Aaron's passion for the sport of wrestling has also brought him to compete all over the world, achieving a bronze medal at the 2011 SEA games. Since then he continues to contribute to the development of the sport by being the federation's Secretary as well as a developmental wrestling coach for his school.
